Final Fantasy 16

Final Fantasy 16 is a game developed by Square Enix and released on june 22nd, 2023. FF16 is a story about an imperfect hero desperately trying to save the country and the ones he loves from a godlike threat. The story touches topics such as slavery, genocide, power struggles and family.
